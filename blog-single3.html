CAPTURING MOMENTS

  • by:Andrea Ilkov
  • date:September 15, 2018

My inspiration to start capturing precious moments was my 6 months old daughter Sofia. I wanted to capture the different stages of her growth, because those moment will never get back. I start calling a few photographers out there and they were ridiculous overpriced, and this is how I decide to start taking pictures on my own.

A moment captured is worth so much more than a moment forgotten. No matter how imperfect the lighting or how basic the camera.

I have the passion, the experience, the creativity and the skills. I’m here to capture whether it’s the shot of your kids first steps or the first dance at your wedding. These moments have so much life and emotion in them. Those are the moments that matter. And when you really capture them, they should be celebrated and remembered.
