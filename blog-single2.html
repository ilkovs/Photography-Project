HOW DID I START TRAVELLING

  • by:Andrea Ilkov
  • date:September 15, 2018

My passion for traveling started way back when I was little girl, my mom always took us to Peru for vacations. It was my favorite place to go because of the different food, people and culture. I remembered I was only 11 years old when I took the first destination that blow my mind. That destination was Ollantaytambo, It is the only Inca city in Peru that still preserves its streets intact, the beauty of this town is magical since it is a living example of a perfect urban plan made by the Incas, with a main square surrounded by streets and water channels that continue to flow from that time. The moment I saw this town I knew there is more to see in this world. There is more out there to explore.

Now traveling has become a part of me. I just have this desire to just pack up my bags and go somewhere, it’s like an addiction that never stops. I like landing in a new place and trying to figure it all out myself. Where to get money. How to speak the language. How to get from point A to point B. I have no problem adapting and molding myself to their culture. I try to explore like a local like I’m part of that city.

Work, Travel, Save, Repeat.

I've been to 20 different countries and to several states and among all those countries I met my soulmate. This is another reason I love traveling. As of today, I'm married to my soul mate and now we have a precious little girl named Sofia. In another article I will explain more about how I met Samuil Ilkov, my better half. The world is big so pack your bags and GO GO GO!
